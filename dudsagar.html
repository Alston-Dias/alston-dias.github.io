The Dudsagar Trek

A nice and cool morning broke through. It was our day to see the beautiful Dudsagar Waterfall. All of us were ready for the trek. We left from Home at 5:30am. It was a one hour drive from Cuncolim to Kolem Station. We reached the station at around 6:35am and were all set to trek to see the beautiful waterfall.

we started walking on rail road. The cloudy day kept us cool and less tired too. We had lots of fun on the way and took breaks too. The passing trains were a bit risky but was fun watching them pass by so close. It took us 3 hours to reach the waterfall.

We all were so amazed looking at the beauty of the waterfall. We started clicking pictures and taking videos. The first thing to never forget ther is the beauty of the waterfall and the second thing is the monkey attack. The monkeys around that place are so furious and hungry that they even attack people with food.

The Dudsagar Waterfall is a worth doing lifetime trek. 28.8KM walking on rail road, the aching legs and the lovely company was a great and worth lifetime experience.
